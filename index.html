Controls

1, p
Pause (set runmode to 'stop')
2, space
Step (set runmode to 'step')
3, p
Run / unpause
r
"Soft" reset the particle playground (run all the systems' reset methods).
R
"Hard" reset the particle playground (reinitialize all the systems, both a "soft" reset and a complete replacement of the particle system data structures).
WASD or Arrow Keys
Move the camera in the horizontal plane
Shift / Ctrl
Move the camera "up" or "down" respectively

Solver
